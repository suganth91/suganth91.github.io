My design process

  • 1. Analyse and WireFrame

    Analyse the usecase, create personas and understand the requirement. Create wireframes based on that in paper or digitally
  • 2. Design and Iterate in Photoshop/Sketch

    Visual design the wireframe and iterate until it feels simple and clutter less to the end user. Design the flow to visualzie the navigation
  • 3. Prototype, get Reviews, Iterate

    Prototype using Marvel or Keynote and let sample users review the flow. Identify the usage difficulties and iterate the prototype. Repeat 2-3
